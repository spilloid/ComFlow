Capabilities

Built for how helpdesks actually work

Every feature is designed around the real rhythm of a support team — fast triage, clear context, and zero dropped balls.

🧠
AI-Powered Triage Live
Swap in any LLM — Claude, GPT-4o, Gemini, a local Ollama model, whatever — and it extracts caller name, company, callback number, intent, urgency, and a plain-English summary. No forms. No lock-in. Structured work items, automatically.
📝
Internal Notes
Add team context to any call. Notes stay attached to the record so the next tech has full history before picking up the phone.
📅
MCP Calendar Integration Soon

📋
SOP Knowledge Base Soon

Smart Queue Routing Soon

Process

Three steps. Zero dropped calls.

ComFlow sits between your phone system and your team, making sure every inbound call becomes an actionable work item.

1
Call comes in
Your telephony system fires a webhook. ComFlow captures the call record, stores the recording reference, and queues it for processing. Nothing is missed.
→
2
AI extracts context
The recording is transcribed by your configured STT engine. Your chosen LLM then pulls out caller name, company, callback number, intent category, urgency level, and a plain summary — all measured and logged.
→
3
Team reviews & resolves
The call lands in the inbox with full context. Technicians review, confirm or correct the AI output, add notes, assign to a queue, and mark resolved — all from one screen.
